Our Story

Acorn was born out of the University of Waterloo's genetic engineering team. Starting out in Velocity Science on-campus, we have sinced moved into the Garage and accumulated funding from AC Jumpstart, Communitech, Velocity and YC Fellowship. On top of that, we are a part of the Creative Destruction Lab and have ties with many investors, scientists, and advisors.


We're passionate about the future of biotechnology and can't wait for what's yet to come. Our dream starts with preservation, but eventually, we hope to expand and take part in further research ourselves.
